Company Profile

Baan Khunyaphat Villas & Residences Co., Ltd. was established in 2021, based on the concept of "Your new way of living".

Our houses are designed to meet the needs of people of all ages, while giving them the freedom to customize the living space from the existing designs and infrastructure according to their own needs.

In addition, environmental technologies such as solar cell systems have been introduced, and we provide installation services to customers interested these systems.
